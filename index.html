About Us

Creative Community

More than a club — a place for artists to connect, create and grow.

The Arts Hub was created to bring together students passionate about visual arts, media, design, photography, music and creative culture. We believe university life becomes more meaningful when creativity is shared through collaboration, conversation and community.

From exhibitions and networking nights to workshops and casual socials, we create spaces where students can explore ideas, showcase work and build lasting friendships.

Benefits of Joining

Empower Student Initiatives

Pitch your own exhibition or workshop idea each semester. Past members have run gallery nights, zine fairs and live mural projects on campus.

Build a Connected Community

100+ Arts students across painting, design, film and photography. Find your people through social events, creative collabs and shared studio sessions.

Enhance Academic Support

Weekly peer study sessions and assignment feedback circles run by senior students — free for all members every semester.

Strengthen Student Success

Past members have landed internships at NGV, ACMI and local studios through our industry networking nights and portfolio review sessions.
